About The Collective

The Up-Cycle Collective is Sheridan's student led club dedicated to sustainable fashion, furniture, and fun. We're all about giving new life to old stuff, whether its repairing worn-out jeans, redesigning a thrifted jacket, or swapping out your old chair for something with flair. We host beginner-friendly workshops, pop-up clothing swaps, DIY repair sessions, and creative challenges where you can learn how to sew, patch, paint, and up-cycle â€” no experience needed. We provide the tools, materials, and guidance. You bring your curiosity and creativity.
